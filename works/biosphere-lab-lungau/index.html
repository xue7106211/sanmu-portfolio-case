Biosphere Lab Lungau

Wayfinding and an interactive kiosk for a UNESCO biosphere reserve visitor lab.

Role Wayfinding & Kiosk
Year 2024
Client Biosphäre Lungau

The lab invites visitors to explore the region's ecology hands-on. Signage and the touchscreen kiosk share one system of pictograms and a green accent drawn from the surrounding alpine meadows.

The kiosk is a static web app running fullscreen — no backend, easy for staff to update on site.

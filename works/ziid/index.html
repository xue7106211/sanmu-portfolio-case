ZIID

A digital archive and publication platform for an interdisciplinary research institute.

Role Platform & Frontend
Year 2023
Client ZIID Zürich

ZIID publishes essays, events and dialogues across disciplines. The platform gives each format its own template while keeping a shared reading experience with strong typographic hierarchy.

Search and filtering run entirely client-side over a static JSON index, keeping the whole archive fast and host-anywhere simple.

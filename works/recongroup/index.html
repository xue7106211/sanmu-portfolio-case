N°01 — Brand & Web

Recongroup

A corporate identity and website for a consulting collective, built around a confident grid and restrained typography.

Role Design & Frontend
Year 2025
Client Recongroup AG

The brief was to make a dense services company feel approachable without losing its authority. We leaned on a strict baseline grid, a single accent color and generous whitespace to let the content breathe.

The frontend is a no-build static site — the same philosophy as this portfolio — so the team can edit copy directly without a toolchain.

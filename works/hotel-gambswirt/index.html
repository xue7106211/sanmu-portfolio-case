N°06 — Hospitality

Hotel Gambswirt

A booking site and brand refresh for a family-run alpine hotel.

Role Brand & Web
Year 2022
Client Hotel Gambswirt

The hotel wanted to feel warm and contemporary while honoring four generations of history. Photography-led pages and a calm serif voice carry that balance from homepage to checkout.

The booking flow is intentionally short — guests reach an enquiry in two steps, which lifted direct reservations away from third-party platforms.

N°02 — Product

Glassland

A glassmorphism design system and marketing site for a SaaS analytics product.

Role Design System
Year 2024
Client Glassland Inc.

Glassland needed a visual language that felt light and modern while staying readable across dense data dashboards. We defined tokens for blur, elevation and translucency, then documented them as reusable components.

The marketing site reuses those same tokens, keeping product and brand in sync without duplicating styles.

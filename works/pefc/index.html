PEFC

An annual sustainability report and microsite for a forest certification body.

Role Editorial & Web
Year 2023
Client PEFC International

The report turns dry certification data into a story about forests and the people who steward them. A warm, paper-like palette and serif headlines give it the feel of a printed annual.

Try the old-school toggle on the home page — this microsite was the direct inspiration for that theme.
